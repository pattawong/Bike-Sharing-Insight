Bike Sharing Insight

During past few year, biking is gaining huge popularity, especially among millennials. From the news, you might hear about new emerging bike sharing service which similar to ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft. However, it not well-known how popular or beneficial bike-sharing service actually is. Therefore by visualizing bike sharing data, we will know exactly what going on with bike sharing trend.
